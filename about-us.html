Our Story

Where every thread tells a story of love, craft, and timeless elegance



The Beginning

Born from a Love for Fashion

Love Threaded was born from a simple dream – to create clothing that celebrates the modern woman while honoring the rich traditions of Indian craftsmanship. Founded in 2020, we started as a small atelier in Mumbai, driven by our passion for quality and design.

Today, we've grown into a beloved brand, but our core values remain unchanged. Every piece that leaves our studio carries with it the dedication of skilled artisans and the promise of exceptional quality.

We believe that fashion should be more than just clothing – it should be a form of self-expression, a source of confidence, and a way to connect with the stories woven into every thread.

Our Mission

Empowering Women Through Fashion

At Love Threaded, we're committed to empowering women – both through the confidence our clothing brings and through our partnerships with women artisans across India.

We work directly with skilled craftswomen, ensuring fair wages and sustainable livelihoods. Every purchase you make contributes to preserving traditional arts while supporting women entrepreneurs.
